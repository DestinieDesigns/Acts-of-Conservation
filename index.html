How to Play

Balance your island’s future by managing three core stats: Available Budget, Environment Health, and Community Trust.

Each turn, roll the dice and move across the board. Every location presents a new challenge or situation.

Every choice affects all three stats. Short-term profit can harm long-term sustainability, and overspending can also lead to collapse.

Token

You are represented by a visible game token on the board.

The token shows your current position and moves with each roll. Follow it to track your progress across the island.

Year System

Each full loop around the board represents one year.

When you pass the Start tile, a Year Summary will appear and your progress and impacts will be reviewed.
