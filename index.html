How To Play

Guide the island one turn at a time.

Roll the dice, move your token, and respond to the issue at each stop. Every choice changes Available Budget, Environment, and Community Trust together.

A full loop around the board counts as one year. Year summaries review your progress, while the final reflection shows what your priorities created over time.
